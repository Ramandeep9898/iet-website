NSIC Rajpura Workshop was conducted at National Small Industries Corporation (NSIC), Rajpura on 11th April 2019 . NSIC provides technical support to MSMEs through 'NSIC Technical Services Centres' (NTSCs) and a number of TICs & LBIs spread across the Country The students were provided with hands-on experience on some live and working projects. They gained practical knowledge on various protection schemes used in domestic appliances, knowledge of Quality Control of electrical applications and testing of electrical appliances as per IEEE standards.

Car from trash was our 2017 project in which a group of engineers decided to make something good out of trash. We came up with the idea of making a car from trash. We looked out for all possible trash that can help them in building the car. After collecting the trash, the main task was How to build a car?. After many Brainstorming sessions we decided to build the car frame from the parts of an old robotics kit that was discarded in the trash and developed its controller using old motors, wires and Arduino.

Online aptitude test was conducted by the IET student chapter knowing the importance of online aptitude quiz for the students to excel and their overall growth. Basically an aptitude test is a systematic means of testing a candidate's abilities to perform specific tasks and react to a range of different situations.
